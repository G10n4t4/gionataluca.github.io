Analysis of a chiller working with R1234ze refrigerant

Evaluate and show how the refrigerating capacity and EER (energy efficiency ratio) of the considered chiller varies changing the operating conditions. The considered chiller works with R1234ze refrigerant and the chosen application for this chiller is to decrease the temperature of a secondary fluid (that is a brine) from 5°C to 0°C in a machinery room and to send it into cabinets of a supermarket to maintain a certain medium temperature (MT) inside for the food conservation (“secondary fluid remote system”). Use of FSS (Frascold selection software) for the choice of the compressor and MATLAB tool.
